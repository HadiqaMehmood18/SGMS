Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How do I submit a grievance?

Simply log in to your account, click on "New Grievance" button, fill out the form with your details, and submit. You can also attach supporting documents.

How long does it take to resolve a grievance?

Most grievances are addressed within 24-48 hours. Critical issues are prioritized and resolved faster. You'll receive email updates throughout the process.

Can I upload attachments with my grievance?

Yes, you can upload images, PDFs, and other documents up to 10MB as proof or supporting evidence for your grievance.

What types of issues can I report?

You can report academic issues, administrative problems, facility concerns, harassment, discrimination, and any other matters affecting your student experience.

Is my information secure?

Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade security measures to protect your personal information and grievance details. Only authorized personnel can access your data.

How do I check the status of my grievance?

Log in to your dashboard and navigate to "My Grievances" section. You can view all your submitted grievances with their current status and progress.
